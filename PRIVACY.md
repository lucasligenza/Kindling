# Privacy Policy — Kindling

**Last updated:** June 28, 2026

## Overview

Kindling is a Chrome extension that transforms article pages into a distraction-free reading experience. It operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What data does Kindling access?

- **Page content:** When you click the Kindling icon, the extension reads the current page's HTML to extract the article text. This content is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any server.

## What data does Kindling store?

- **Settings:** Your font size and margin width preferences are saved locally using Chrome's storage API.
- **Highlights:** Text passages you highlight are saved locally using Chrome's storage API, keyed by page URL.

All stored data remains on your device. Nothing is transmitted externally.

## What data does Kindling collect or share?

None. Kindling does not:
- Collect any personal information
- Track your browsing activity
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or telemetry
- Contain ads

## Third-party services

Kindling does not integrate with any third-party services.

Any changes will be reflected in this document on the project's GitHub repository.
